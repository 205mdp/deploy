E:2.14 KIDNAP/EXTORTION

In the extraordinary event of a kidnapping of an associate (or a guest) the Division Crisis Representative or one of the Corporate Contact representatives must be notified immediately per the Crisis Response & Communications Plan. Starwood Hotels & Resorts has a specific policy regarding how kidnappings or extortions must be handled and who is to be involved. That policy is considered too confidential to put into this document.

For contact information see the Preparation Section in this manual.
