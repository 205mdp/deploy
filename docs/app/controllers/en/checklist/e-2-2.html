E:2.2 CIVIL DISTURBANCE

A civil disturbance is the action of a group of people demonstrating or protesting for an issue or cause. The disturbance can occur in or near the hotel; it may be related to a guest or group using the facilities. A civil disturbance can be peaceful – or violent (see below). In any event, the immediate concern is always the protection of hotel guests, associates and property.

Any associate noticing a civil disturbance should immediately contact Security. The Security Office will then be used as an initial Command Post. A fully equipped Command Post may later be needed.

Unless a disturbance turns violent and presents an immediate danger to guests and associates, DO NOT attempt to forcibly disperse protestors. That activity should be left to law enforcement authorities.

It is also important that associates not engage protestors in debate or shouting matches. Media may be present and confrontation will only tend to escalate news coverage. In other words, don’t create a media event.
