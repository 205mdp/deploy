E:1.1 ASSISTING DISABLED GUESTS

Many emergencies will require special assistance for guests with disabilities. The most obvious are emergencies which require evacuation, search and rescue or shelter-in-place.

Each Hotel must develop a plan for identifying and providing assistance to guests with special needs in the event of any emergency or crisis.
