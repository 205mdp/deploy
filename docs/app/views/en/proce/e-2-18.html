E:2.18 SUSPICIOUS ITEM OR PACKAGE

If you become aware of an item or package that seems suspicious or out-of-place, notify Security immediately. Do not touch or move the item. Move guests and personnel as far away as possible from the item.

SECURITY

  • Respond immediately to the location of the item.
  • Cordon off the area and move guest and associates away from the area.
  • If possible, determine the nature of the item without touching it.
  • Do not shake, open or empty the contents of any suspicious envelope or package.
  • Do not sniff, touch, taste or look closely at it or at any contents that may have spilled.
  • Do not carry the package or envelope, show it to others or allow others to examine it.
  • If the item must be handled or opened in order to determine what it is, contact the appropriate law enforcement authorities.
  • If the suspicious item is believed to be a bomb, follow Bomb Threat (17.1) procedures
  • Assist law enforcement as needed.
  • Respectfully question associates or guest who may be aware of where the item came from. Report any evidence or important information to law enforcement.
  • Complete a report on the incident that documents what was found and any evidence that could help in an investigation.

GENERAL MANAGER

  • Ensure guests and associates do not go near the item.
  • Assist Security as needed.
  • Contact the appropriate authorities if Security has not done this already.
  • Reassure guests that everything is being done to protect their safety.
