E:2.2.1 PEACEFUL DEMONSTRATION

OPERATOR

  • Call Security with the details of the demonstration and approximate number of people involved.
  • Notify the Emergency Response Team of the disturbance and its location.
  • Be prepared to contact local emergency services (9-1-1 in the U.S.) for assistance if needed.

SECURITY

  • Assist the Director of Security in his/her duties (outlined below).
  • Take a loud speaker (bullhorn) to the scene to be used if needed.
  • If necessary, contact local emergency services (9-1-1 in the U.S.). Give details of the demonstration, such as the location, number of people involved, and general demeanor of the protestors.
  • Monitor the situation during the demonstration, but in a way that does not threaten, intimidate or provoke the protestors. DO NOT insert yourself into the demonstration or attempt to disrupt it.

DIRECTOR OF SECURITY

  • With the assistance of Security personnel, attempt to identify the protest groupâ€™s leader(s) and determine the reason for the disturbance.
  • Identify yourself and politely ask the protest leader to relocate the protest off the property.
  • If the request to relocate is refused, ask the cooperation of the group leader in not entering the hotel, blocking the entrances or disturbing the activities of hotel guests.
  • If the demonstration is directed against a guest or group in the hotel, attempt to locate the guest or leader of the group and inform them of the situation.
  • Be prepared to place hotel associates at the entrances and exits in order to help ensure the safety of guests and hotel staff.
  • Contact local law enforcement.
