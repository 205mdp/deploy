E:2.22.4 WINTER STORMS/SEVERE FREEZES

During severely inclement winter weather, ensure the safety of all guests and associates by discouraging them from leaving the Hotel. Listen to news reports and stay informed of weather conditions. Answer any guest questions.

If an associate is scheduled to report to work during icy winter weather, listen to news reports to determine the safety of travel. If it is unsafe to leave home, call the Hotel and inform your supervisor.

GENERAL MANAGER

  • Receive weather news reports and stay informed of weather conditions.
  • Assign an associate to call the airport and receive travel delay information to relay to guests.
  • Using the public address system or available communication process let all guests know the status of the weather and update them regularly. Encourage them to stay safely in the Hotel.
  • Have the transportation dispatcher instruct transportation associates to return to the Hotel immediately if they are able to do so. Do not allow other Hotel vehicles to leave the premises.
  • Set-up a “Hospitality Area” for associates and Hotel visitors who are stranded at the Hotel. Instruct Housekeeping to provide blankets, sheets, towels and cots if necessary.
  • Obtain a report of disabled guests. Regularly send an associate to check on them to make sure they do not require assistance.
  • Assess staffing needs and determine if staff should be held over, particularly if relief staff may be unable to reach the Hotel.

DIRECTOR OF SECURITY

  • Assign Security personnel to monitor Hotel entrances and exits to discourage guests and associates from leaving.
  • Have Security Officers patrol the Hotel to ensure guest safety and comfort.
  • Establish a first-aid room to handle any emergencies in the event that Emergency Services can not reach the Hotel.
  • Answer guest questions.
  • Assist the General Manager as needed.

SECURITY

  • Patrol the Hotel to ensure guest safety and comfort.
  • Report any issues or problems to the Director of Security.
  • Assist the Director of Security with establishing a first-aid room.
  • Answer guest questions.

ENGINEERING

  • Establish key areas to drip the faucets in the Hotel to keep water moving through the pipes and prevent pipe breaks.
  • Protect fire suppression sprinkler heads, using space heaters if necessary.
  • Ensure an ample supply of snow & ice melt and apply as directed.
  • In addition, sprinkle sand on pavement areas in front of Hotel entrances and exits.
  • Have associates use snow shovels to clear all entrances and exits. Ensure associates are suitably attired.
  • All exits must be kept clear of obstruction.
  • Ensure all heating equipment and other utilities are working properly.
  • Schedule pre-arranged vendor for snow & ice removal.
  • Check areas around downspout drains. Check all known low areas where water may accumulate and freeze.
  • Check all outdoor walkways.
  • If you have an event hall entrance, but no event taking place, take automatic door off automatic.
