E:2.3 COMMUNICABLE DISEASE

If an illness or any other situation requires that guests or associates should be prevented from contact with others, contact Security and the General Manager as quickly as possible. Immediately direct the affected persons to a comfortable room away from high traffic areas.

If the illness has spread to the other guests or is of a serious nature, immediately contact County/State Health Officials or the Center for Disease Control (if in U.S.). If terrorist activity is suspected, immediately contact law enforcement officials.

Be aware of local regulatory requirements for reporting the event. Information must be carefully managed.

The Hotel must immediately contact the Legal Department and the Division Crisis Representative for direction.

GENERAL MANAGER

  • Contact the Legal Department and the Division Crisis Representative.
  • Assure all guests that everything is being done for their health and safety. Encourage guests to remain calm. Regularly update the guests with a progress report as the issue is addressed.
  • Contact County/State Health Officials or the Center for Disease Control (if in U.S.) as soon as necessary to prevent the problem from expanding.
  • Maintain guest/associate privacy but cooperate with health department officials.
  • Answer guest inquiries and keep them informed about what is happening, instruct non-affected guests to keep away from affected areas or people.
  • Inform Security of the problem and request their assistance.
  • If the Hotel seems to be affected, take direction from local health officials for cleaning areas.
  • Provide the affected guests with food and drinks.
  • Track and record all costs incurred for insurance purposes and file a report.

SECURITY

  • Secure affected areas and keep unaffected guests from these parts of the Hotel.
  • KEEP FIRE EXITS OPEN, but place a guard at each location.
  • Answer guest questions and provide assistance to guests as needed.
  • Assist medical or law enforcement teams as needed.
  • Ensure that guests or associates do not leave or enter the Hotel or affected area without the approval of Health Officials.
