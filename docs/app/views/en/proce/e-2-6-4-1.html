E:2.6.4.1 DEATH OR SUICIDE

OPERATOR

  • Call Security with details about the discovery of the body and its location.
  • Ask the Emergency Response Team to immediately contact the Director of Security. Do not announce over the radio the nature of the situation.

SECURITY

  • Contact local emergency services (9-1-1 in the U.S.), giving all details known.
  • See instructions for the Director of Security (below) and assist as needed.
  • Ensure that no unauthorized persons enter the cordoned off area.
  • Keep on-lookers away from the scene in a calm, respectful manner.
  • Once the deceased has been removed, double-lock the room until police release the room for clean up. (Note: Inventory of the deceasedâ€™s property shall be done by police and documented by Security).
  • Prepare the appropriate incident report. Include photographs or diagrams if available.

DIRECTOR OF SECURITY

  • Respond to the scene. Do not assume that the person is deceased; resuscitation efforts may be necessary.
  • DO NOT TOUCH OR ALLOW ANYONE TO DISTURB ANYTHING AT THE SCENE.
  • Cordon off the area and post Security personnel to ensure that only authorized persons are allowed to enter the scene.
  • Keep a log of all persons entering the scene, including times.
  • Obtain as much information as possible for authorities:
    • Who discovered the body or witnessed the death?
    • Time of discovery or death?
    • Name and address of deceased, as well as persons knowing the deceased?
  • Dispatch a member of the Emergency Response Team to meet police and fire department personnel responding to the call.
  • If the individual is a registered guest, have the Front Office print out a folio and obtain a copy of the registration card for authorities.
  • Pull room lock information for the entire period the guest was registered and give to authorities.
  • If clean up of body fluids is necessary, ensure that Bloodborne Pathogen guidelines are followed.
  • Assist local authorities as needed.
  • Notify senior management.
  • Confirm with local authorities that they will notify next of kin.

GENERAL MANAGER

  • Refer media inquires to law enforcement.
  • Contact the Division Representative to the Corporate Crisis Response Team.

HUMAN RESOURCES

  • Contact EAP and arrange for deployment of onsite grief counselors.
  • Advise associates of EAP and counseling resources available.
