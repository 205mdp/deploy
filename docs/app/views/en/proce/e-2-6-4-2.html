E:2.6.4.2 ATTEMPTED OR THREATENED SUICIDE

OPERATOR

  • Immediately contact Security with details of the situation, including location and description of the individual.
  • Ask the Emergency Response Team to contact the Operator immediately. Do not announce over the radio the nature of the situation.

SECURITY

  • Contact police emergency (9-1-1 in U.S.), giving all details known.
  • See instructions for the Director of Security (below) and assist as needed.
  • Cordon off the area and keep on-lookers away from the scene.
  • Prepare the appropriate incident report.

DIRECTOR OF SECURITY

  • Immediately respond to the scene. Address the individual threatening suicide in a calm and soothing manner until experts from the police or fire department arrive.
  • Do not put yourself at risk.
  • Attempt to isolate the surrounding area.
  • Dispatch a member of the Emergency Response Team to meet police and fire department personnel responding to the call.
  • If the individual is a registered guest, have the Front Office print out a folio and obtain a copy of the registration card for authorities.
  • Assist local authorities as needed.
  • Notify senior management.
  • Confirm with local authorities that they will notify relatives or associates of the individual in duress.
  • If clean up of body fluids is necessary, ensure that Bloodborne Pathogen guidelines are followed.

GENERAL MANAGER

  • Refer media inquires to law enforcement.
  • Contact the Division Representative to the Corporate Crisis Response Team.

HUMAN RESOURCES

  • Contact EAP and arrange for deployment of onsite grief counselors.
  • Advise associates of EAP and counseling resources available.
